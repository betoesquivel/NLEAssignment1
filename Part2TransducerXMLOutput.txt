======================== CURRENCY PARSER =========================
Welcome to the currency parser!
Send a file as a parameter or allow me to scrape a bbc website.
Send -xml as a parameter at the end, if you want the output as xml.
Usage: perl currency_transducer.pl <OPTIONAL_FILE_TO_PARSE> -xml
